My Adventure Details


  • NATURE

    Okay, picture this: Rome hit the jackpot with peace! Imagine tons of trade thanks to safe roads, emperors spending like crazy on awesome buildings, and everyone getting jobs. It wasn't perfect, but for a long time, Rome boomed – like a super rich empire thanks to that sweet, sweet prosperity!

    ANTIQUITIES

    Rome's like a giant time capsule! Huge Colosseum and stuff, plus houses in Pompeii perfectly preserved by a volcano, like a snapshot of their lives. They even have tons of cool statues and pottery, whispering secrets of the past!

    PROSPERITY

    Rome got crazy rich during the Pax Romana, this super long peacetime. Trade exploded with safe roads, and emperors showered the cities with gold, building everywhere! Boom! Roman Empire = super wealthy!
