My Adventure Details


  • NATURE

    Moscow might seem like a big concrete jungle at first, but trust me, there's a surprising amount of green hiding in plain sight! Huge parks like Gorky Park are perfect for picnicking under towering birch trees, and in spring, the whole city explodes with colorful lilacs and tulips. Even in winter, the snow blanketing the parks creates a magical wonderland, perfect for snowball fights or a cozy walk with a hot cocoa.

    ANTIQUITIES

    My friend Olya was geeking out about this exhibit she saw - ancient treasures right in the Moscow Kremlin! Apparently they have beautiful jewelry and even weapons from way back when. Super cool, right? Makes you think about all the history hiding under this big city.

    PROSPERITY

    Olya keeps bragging about how Moscow's booming! Apparently, fancy coffee shops are popping up everywhere and everyone seems to be rocking new clothes. Sounds like the city's got some serious cash flowing!
