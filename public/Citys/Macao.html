My Adventure Details


  • NATURE

    Macoa might be a bustling city, but it has some pretty surprising natural beauty spots too! We went hiking in Coloane which has these awesome hiking trails that take you through lush greenery and past these cool rock formations. It's pretty amazing to see these giant rocks next to the skyscrapers in the distance! Another day we kayaked in the Inner Harbor. The water was so calm and it was neat seeing the city skyline from a different perspective. Macau is definitely more than just casinos!

    ANTIQUITIES

    "You wouldn't believe it! Macoa might be a glitzy city now, but hidden beneath the skyscrapers are whispers of the past. There's this whole network of underground tunnels rumored to be from the Portuguese era, and there are even these beautiful old churches with intricate carvings that make you feel like you've stepped back in time. It's like a whole other world waiting to be explored!"

    PROSPERITY

    "Ugh, you wouldn't believe how lucky Macoa is! Her family just got promoted at work, like, both of them! They're talking fancy vacations and a whole house renovation. It's raining money over there, I swear. Makes me wonder what good karma she has that I don't..."
