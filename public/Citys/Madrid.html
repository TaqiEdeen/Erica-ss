My Adventure Details


  • NATURE

    Madrid might be a bustling city, but it has its green corners too! Huge parks like Retiro with its crystal palace and boating lake are perfect for escaping the crowds. There's even Casa de Campo, a sprawling forest park on the city's edge, where you can hike, bike, or even spot deer! It's like a whole other world hidden within Madrid.

    ANTIQUITIES

    Madrid's like a history book come alive! The big museum has awesome Egyptian stuff, but even the streets hide secrets. Imagine, an ancient gate from the city walls just hanging out in a modern building! It's crazy!

    PROSPERITY

    Madrid's like a total boss city! It's buzzing with business and culture, you know, like fancy museums and trendy shops. You can practically feel the energy in the air!
