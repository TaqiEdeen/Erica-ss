I'm Erica,

Travel wasn't always my middle name! Stuck in a desk job for years, I dreamt of adventure but never quite took the leap. Then, a canceled wedding (crazy, right?) became an unexpected blessing. With a plane ticket in one hand and a backpack in the other, I embarked on a solo trip to Southeast Asia.

That backpacking adventure ignited a fire in me. While I love the comfort of a nice hotel sometimes, there's a thrill in exploring off-the-beaten-path destinations and unexpected cultural experiences. I wouldn't call myself a hardcore budget traveler, but I'm definitely resourceful. Finding the best street food stalls and unique local experiences is what makes travel exciting for me.

One of my most humbling experiences was getting lost (joyfully!) in the labyrinthine alleyways of Marrakech. After a few wrong turns and a lot of laughter with friendly locals, I stumbled upon a hidden gem - a tiny courtyard cafe with the most delicious mint tea I've ever tasted.

While Southeast Asia holds a special place in my heart, every destination offers something special. From the awe-inspiring natural beauty of Iceland to the vibrant chaos of Ho Chi Minh City, I find myself constantly surprised and delighted.

For aspiring travel bloggers, my biggest advice is to just start! Don't wait for everything to be perfect. Document your adventures, share your honest experiences, and focus on what makes you unique. The travel community is full of supportive people who love to share stories and inspire each other.

So, that's me, Erica, the unexpected explorer. Come along with me as I share my travel mishaps, hidden gems, and unexpected adventures!
